Limbitless
Orthotics & Prosthetics

Our clinic is dedicated to providing Orthotic and Prosthetic devices from head to toe. We are committed to working with patients until we reach the highest level of functionality possible. We understand that devices won't function like a part of your body unless they feel like a part of your body. At Limbetless we keep the majority of the fabrication process in house to ensure quality devices are delivered and all goals are met.
