Orthotics

There is not an orthotic device that we can not make. We will make devices for the lower extremity, back, upper extremity, neck, and head. No matter your cause for need, we have seen it and have experience with it. Our devices are made with state of the art technology by highly trained professionals. If we don't make it, nobody makes it.

Prosthetics

We have made prosthetic devices for young and old, big and small, and anything in between. No matter your level of amputation, (transtibial, transfemoral, hip disarticulation, transradial transhumeral, shoulder disarticulation) we can help you. We use state of the art technology not limited to microprocessors, titanium, and carbon fiber. Our specialty is adjustable sockets, because we understand the daily fitting challenges that come with utilizing a prosthesis.
